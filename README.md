# Gallery of Insights
## *Making things better; making better things.*

I am a compulsive taker—and sender—of notes about interfaces. 

To me, "interface" can mean any way you interact with systems or devices, from a perfectly balanced steering wheel to the invisible logic of a store checkout line; from the elegance of a crisply written manual to the frustration of a voice-driven menu where no option quite fits.

As a writer and editor, I use this practice to constantly sharpen my own work. I also hope my observations—the gripes, the praise, and the questions—spark ideas for the people building the next, better versions of each. 

Some of these thoughts have been drawn from more than a decade of online posting logs; others go back much further, starting out as scribbles on napkins, sticky notes, or book margins. You may not agree with all of them, but I bet you’ll nod along to many.

### The Tactile Authority of the Steering Wheel
**The Principle:** Control surfaces should provide "Weight" proportional to the consequence of the action.

**The Friction:** Modern "drive-by-wire" systems and capacitive touch buttons on steering wheels remove the physical resistance that tells a driver's brain how much input they are giving. When a steering wheel feels like a video game controller, the "interface" between man and machine loses its high-fidelity feedback loop.

**The Resolution:** Return to physical, weighted resistance for critical inputs. A steering wheel should fight back just enough to remind you that you are moving two tons of metal.

### Get the weight right -- and lighter isn't always better

**The Principle:** Control surfaces should provide "Weight" proportional to the consequence of the action.

**The Friction:** Modern "drive-by-wire" systems and capacitive touch buttons on steering wheels remove the physical resistance that tells a driver's brain how much input they are giving. 

**The Resolution:** Return to physical, weighted resistance for critical inputs. A steering wheel should fight back just enough to remind you that you are moving two tons of metal.
